About

My name is Vlada. I paint on natural slate — white, gold, and occasionally red on dark stone. White is matter, gold is light, red is life. Each piece starts with observation: people, nature, moments worth keeping. I'm not a trained artist. I'm a physicist and project manager who sees the world in systems and patterns. That's what shows up in the work. Fosforicit means phosphorescent — light emerging from darkness. That's the point. Originals are available — inquire below.
